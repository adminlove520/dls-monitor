Discover the Benefits of Medicare Advantage (Part C)

What Medicare Advantage Plans May Include:

  • Comprehensive Coverage: Includes all benefits and services covered under Part A and Part B.
  • Prescription Drug Coverage: Most plans include Medicare Part D.
  • Extra Benefits: Such as vision, hearing, dental, and wellness programs.
  • Out-of-Pocket Maximum: Annual limit on your out-of-pocket costs for covered services.
